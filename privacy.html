Pakku: Learn English Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-02-12

1. Introduction

Welcome to "Pakku: Learn English" (the "App"), owned and operated by Pakku ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address. If you sign in using OpenID providers like Google, Facebook, Amazon, or Apple, we will access your profile information such as name, email address, and profile picture with your consent.
  • Application Data: We collect information about your progress in lessons, user preferences for the application UI, and language learning choices.
  • Payment Information: We do not collect or store your financial information like credit card details. All payments are handled securely through Google Payment Services.

3. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize your language learning experience.
  • To track your progress and achievements.
  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To analyze app usage and improve our services.
  • To communicate with you about app updates and promotions (with your consent).
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Legal Bases for Information Collection

We collect and process your information based on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: We process your information to fulfill our contractual obligations to provide you with the language learning services you requested.
  • Consent: We process your information for marketing and promotional purposes only with your consent.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process your information for analytics and improvement purposes based on our legitimate interest in developing and maintaining a valuable language learning app.

5. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with any third party except:

  • Service providers who help us operate and maintain the app, such as Google Firebase for data storage. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Law enforcement or other government officials when required by law.
  • As part of a merger, acquisition, or other business transaction.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the services. We will delete your information upon your request or if your account remains inactive for an extended period.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to access and obtain a copy of your personal information.
  • Erasure: You have the right to request the deletion of your information.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at support@pakku.app.

8. International Information Transfer

Your information may be transferred and stored in the United States, where Google Firebase is located. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure the security of your information during transfer and storage.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We take extra precautions to protect the privacy of young users. If you are under 13, we require consent from your parent or guardian to use the app. Parents or guardians can contact us at support@pakku.app to review or delete their child's information.

10. Third-Party Services

We use OpenID authentication providers (Google, Facebook, Amazon, Apple) for logging into our app. Please review their privacy policies as they may collect personal information.

We do not share your PII with any third-party services, except as required to provide our app services or as required by law.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@pakku.app

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy
